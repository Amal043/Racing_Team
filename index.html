Our Story

Built on Passion,
Driven by Precision

Founded in 2009, the NIT Jamshedpur Drift Racing Team is the institution's flagship motorsport engineering program. What began as twelve passionate engineers has grown into a 40+ member powerhouse competing at the national Formula Student stage.

We design, build, and race single-seat formula cars entirely from scratch — developing deep expertise across mechanical engineering, aerodynamics, embedded electronics, and computational design.

Our journey spans over a decade of innovation — from our first combustion prototype to pioneering electric vehicle development at NIT Jamshedpur.

Our Legacy

2009
Foundation
Team Drift Racing Founded
12 passionate NIT Jamshedpur engineers form the Drift Racing Team with a single mission — compete at the national Formula Student stage.

First Podium
National Top-3 Finish
DR-13 earns the team's first-ever top-3 at Formula Bharat, validating years of iterative development.
